Natural Landscapes

Natural landscapes are geographical formations in their purest state, with minimal human intervention. These include mountains, forests, rivers, lakes, deserts, and coasts, among others. They reflect the biodiversity and majesty of nature, providing not only a refuge for a wide variety of flora and fauna but also spaces of aesthetic beauty and tranquility for humans. Natural landscapes are essential for ecological balance and the well-being of the planet, playing a crucial role in environmental conservation and sustainable development.
